About Me

Enjoy exploring latest technology and trying out new potentials. Having solid experience in programming, I built up various hobby projects in IoT applications and apps on iOS/Android. It has always been a pleasure to program microcontroller with internet connectivity that makes life easier. Docker then became one of my favourite tools on backend deployment. At this point, I was fascinated by beauty of embedded system and further joined master program in Uni Freiburg in 2018. Later in 2019, I found an internship opportunity in UI/UX Advanced Engineering department at Daimler that prototypes on latest UI concept. Working closely with graphic designer, we bring the design to live. More recently, I am working on master thesis and getting ready for next adventure.
